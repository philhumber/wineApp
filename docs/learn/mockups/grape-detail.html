Did You Know?
Pinot Noir is called "the heartbreak grape" because it is so difficult to grow. It has over 1,000 known clones -- more than any other grape variety. A mutation of Pinot Noir gave us Pinot Gris (grey skin) and Pinot Blanc (white skin).
