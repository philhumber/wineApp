Notes
Incredible depth. Dark cassis and violet on the nose, with this silky tannin structure that just kept unfolding. The kind of wine that makes you sit in silence for a moment. Shared with Sarah at our anniversary dinner. The sommelier suggested decanting for two hours and he was absolutely right - it opened up beautifully over the course of the evening.
